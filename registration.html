International Conference on Materials for Dielectric Applications MDA 2014

Registration

Please note that due to budgetary constraints, we are unable to provide any financial assistance. All participants and all the invited speakers are requested to pay the registration fee. The registration fee for the conference is fixed at an affordable rate.
The registration fee for the conference includes registration materials, conference proceedings, lunches, dinners, refreshments, the special conference dinner, cultural program.
Registration fees do not include transportation expenses, or hotel accommodations.
Conference registration cannot be confirmed until payment is received.
Participation is not guaranteed until full payment of the registration fee is received.
The conference program may be subject to changes.
Payments will be refunded if the conference will be canceled by the organizer. In that case, the organizer will have no further liability to the client. Registrations remain valid if the conference has to be postponed.
To publish a paper, one of the authors must pay the registration fee. Please note that a regular registration entitles an author to publish max two papers. All papers must be presented during conference.
Reduced rate is applicable for students. Confirmation should be sent by email together with the registration form.
