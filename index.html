International Conference on Materials for Dielectric Applications MDA 2014

We warmly invite you to attend and to contribute to International Conference on Materials for Dielectric Applications MDA 2014

The International Conference on Materials for Dielectric Applications MDA 2014 will be held September 10th - September 12th 2014 in Wroclaw, Poland.

Conference is organized by:

Wroclaw University of Technology, Faculty of Electrical Engineering, Wroclaw, Poland
International and Interuniversity Centre for Nanoscience and Nanotechnology, Mahatma Gandhi University, Kottayam, Kerala, India
International Conference on Materials for Dielectric Applications is an interdisciplinary research-oriented meeting to allow researchers from different countries direct exchange experiences, discuss new opportunities and development in the broad sense of dielectric materials. It is addressed to physicists, chemists,material scientists and electrical engineers who are engaged in the research and practical applications of such materials.
Conference Program Committee composed of highly recognized experts from many different countries around the world provides a high scientific level of that meeting.


Publication

We are glad to inform you that the Editors of prestigious Journals have agreed to publish peer reviewed papers presented at the conference as special issue.
