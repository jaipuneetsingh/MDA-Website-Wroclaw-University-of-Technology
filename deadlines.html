ABSTRACT

For abstract submission,
1. Please sign up for an account and register as author at: https://www.easychair.org/conferences/?conf=mda2014
2. Login with your username and your password.
3. Fill out the required fields, tick the Abstract Only box and send abstract.

The abstract should be in English with following content:
Title of the paper
Principal author and co-authors, including their affiliations
Keywords
Main text (up to 2 pages)
